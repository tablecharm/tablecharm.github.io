About Us

Table Charm Direct is a Southern African multi-level marketing company offering a wide range of household, health, fashion and beauty products through a trusted direct selling network. We provide an opportunity for individuals to earn an income by selling products and building a business.

Our extensive product catalog includes tableware, personal care items, cosmetics, fragrances, fashion wear, and health products. We operate on direct selling model, where independent representatives sell products directly to consumers. The company also offers a multi-level marketing structure for those who wish to build a larger business by recruiting others.

  • Direct selling with personal service
  • Multi-level opportunity to grow your income
  • Focus on family-friendly, everyday essentials
